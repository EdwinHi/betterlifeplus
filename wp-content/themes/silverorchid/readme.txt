How to install:
--------------
Go to Appearance > Themes, Copy the theme folder 'putte' in wp-content/themes/ and activate it.


How to use Featured Image:
-----------------
Open the editor of the post where you want to display the featured image and locate the 'Featured Image' module in the bottom right corner under Page Attributes. Click 'Set featured image'.
You will see an uploading screen. Follow the on-screen prompts to choose the image from your computer and upload it or choose it from one of the images already in your Media Library. Once the image has successfully been uploaded, look underneath the sizing options, and click Use as featured image.
Don’t forget to click Update on your post/page to save your changes. Your featured image is now set!
